--- correct tables.py ---

Used to interpolate all rows and fill missing data across all tables. 


--- Analyze and remove duplicates.py ---

Analyze and print a report of time gaps by exporting db tables to csv . 

- analyze_intervals() takes a tolerance_seconds parameter.
- Prompts for the deletion of duplicates (> 5s)
- Clears csv files from disk

** Only use if gaps between timestamps are already accurate, else run correct_tables.py first **
